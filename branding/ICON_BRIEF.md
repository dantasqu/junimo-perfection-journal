# Junimo Perfection Journal Icon Brief

## Core Idea

An open Stardew-style book or journal icon with a real Junimo inside the pages.

The Junimo should feel like the heart of the journal:
- centered in the open pages
- warm, friendly, magical
- glowing in a perfection-themed honey gold / amber tone

## Visual Direction

- Use an open book inspired by Stardew Valley's in-game book and journal icon style.
- The book should feel hand-made, cozy, slightly chunky, and game-like.
- Pages should be cream or parchment, not bright white.
- Cover and trim can use walnut brown with soft golden details.
- The Junimo should be clearly readable at small size.

## Perfection Color

Primary perfection glow:
- soft honey gold
- slightly magical amber

Suggested palette:
- Honey gold: `#E7BE58`
- Magical amber: `#D89A3C`
- Parchment cream: `#F5E6BF`
- Walnut brown: `#7B4B24`
- Moss green accent: `#6D8F49`

## Composition

- Open book viewed front-on or at a slight Stardew-like angle
- Junimo appears inside the center fold or rising slightly from the pages
- A subtle golden glow spills across the paper
- Keep the silhouette simple enough to work as an app icon

## Avoid

- hyper-realistic rendering
- neon yellow gold
- dark or spooky magic vibes
- cluttered props around the book
- tiny unreadable details

## One-Sentence Prompt

Create a cozy Stardew Valley-style app icon of an open storybook journal with parchment pages and a glowing golden Junimo inside, using soft honey gold and magical amber tones, with a clear readable silhouette and warm game-like charm.
